About this app

Welcome to Street Saarthi, your companion in the bustling streets of India! Developed by the National Association of Street Vendors of India (NASVI), this app is designed to empower street vendors by providing them with crucial information, support, and a direct line to the NASVI team.

Key Features:

📢 Instant Notifications: Stay informed about the latest schemes, initiatives, and training programs tailored for street vendors. Receive timely updates that matter to your business.

🤝 Connect with NASVI: Bridge the gap between street vendors and NASVI effortlessly. Communicate directly with the NASVI team, ensuring a seamless flow of information and support.

📣 Notice Board: Access important notices right from your smartphone. Never miss an announcement that could positively impact your street vending experience.

📞 Complaint Redressal: Report issues and concerns with ease. Street Saarthi provides a direct channel for lodging complaints, ensuring that your voice is heard, and solutions are delivered promptly.

🌐 Empowering Street Vendors: Street Saarthi is more than an app; it's a platform dedicated to the growth and well-being of street vendors across India. Join the community and be a part of the change.

Download Street Saarthi today and embark on a journey towards a more connected and empowered street vending experience!
